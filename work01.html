The Syllables of a Forgotten Room

Moving Image with Stereo Sound, 4:35 min


Weaving moving images, sound and scent, this work constructs a liminal space hovering between collapse and rebirth. It invites audiences to this haunting meditation—a chance to shed modernity's weight and dwell in a forgotten, mother-like realm, where being lost is the destination, and presence becomes the ritual of rebirth.
