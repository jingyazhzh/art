About Me

Jingya Zhang is a multidisciplinary artist working at the intersection of illustration, textiles, 3D design, and sound. Building upon her professional experience in picture books and commercial illustration, she integrates digital fabrication to expand the spatial and motion aspects of visual storytelling.

Thinking with tactile textures and disrupted planes, she constructs surreal spatial narratives that resist rationality’s disembodied gaze. Her works reawaken ambiguity, interconnectedness, and fluidity via poetic interventions in materiality.
